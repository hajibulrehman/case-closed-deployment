Murder / Serial Killers
The Charley Project — massive database of unsolved murders and missing persons
Murder Accountability Project — FBI homicide data, downloadable CSVs
Wikipedia: List of serial killers — structured, well-cited entries you can copy directly into your admin panel
Crime Museum — detailed write-ups on famous cases (Bundy, Dahmer, BTK, Gacy, etc.)
Missing Persons
NamUs (National Missing and Unidentified Persons System) — US government database, searchable and free
NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children) — child cases specifically
Doe Network — international unidentified remains database

Genocide Watch — country-by-country reports
United States Holocaust Memorial Museum — Holocaust and modern genocides
Srebrenica Memorial — detailed Bosnia genocide records
Khmer Rouge Tribunal documents — Cambodia genocide court records, public

CourtListener (Free Law Project) — millions of US court opinions, free API
PACER — US federal court records (small per-page fee)
The Marshall Project — investigative criminal justice journalism with data

Murderpedia — encyclopedia of murderers, very structured, easy to copy

All That's Interesting — True Crime — well-written summaries with sources
